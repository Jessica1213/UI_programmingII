Question-based Game

There are some questions. Their answers are animations that move. You should drag the answer image and drop it on to the basket.

Memory Game

Four images are shown to you on four cards. Then the cards rotate and you can not see the images. Then one question pop ups and one of those images is the answer. You just need to go on the answer card.

Game - Memory

If you want to have a photo as your avatar, it is simple. You see a button in the middle of the page and you can simply click it and take your photo.

Scores

Green stars come in the left corner of pages when you answer a question correctly. If it is not correct, a red star pop ups one second in order to show that your answer is wrong.
